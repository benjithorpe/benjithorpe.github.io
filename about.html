About Me

I consider myself a forever student, eager to build on my academic foundations and try to stay in tune with latest technology conventions through the tech communities, articles and practice.

I am determined to learn and understand everything I can about technology. My aim is to be a very good Back-end Engineer/Developer and hopefully someday a Cloud Engineer.

I have been in the learning process since I developed passion for programming in high school and have been involved in some tech communities for the past 10 months.

However, my interests goes beyond tech. I love fiction, tech movies, chess (but can't play). I also love listening to advice, success stories, guides and tips from people on every aspect of life.

I also try my best to help others by sharing good articles, tutorials, books and helpful methods/skills used by many developers.

I believe that determination, passion, hard work and learning from others are the key to success.
